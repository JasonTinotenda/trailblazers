OUR FRAMEWORK

Everything You Need for a Life of Impact

Our ministry is built on these core pillars, designed to foster holistic growth and equip you to be a catalyst in your world.

Spiritual Impact
Nurturing a generation hungry for God, building spiritually and doctrinally sound young people through worship, prayer, and study.

START YOUR JOURNEY

A New Beginning

The most important decision you can ever make is to accept Jesus Christ as your Lord and Savior. Here are the steps to begin your relationship with Him.

Steps to Salvation

1. Acknowledge God's Plan
God loves you and has a plan for your life. He desires a relationship with you.
"For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life." - John 3:16
2. Recognize Our Sin
We have all sinned and fallen short of God's perfect standard. This sin separates us from Him.
"For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." - Romans 3:23
3. Repent and Turn to God
Repentance means turning away from our sin and turning towards God.
"Repent, then, and turn to God, so that your sins may be wiped out, that times of refreshing may come from the Lord." - Acts 3:19
4. Believe in Jesus Christ
Believe that Jesus died on the cross for your sins and was raised from the dead. This is the ultimate act of love and sacrifice.
"If you declare with your mouth, 'Jesus is Lord,' and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved." - Romans 10:9
5. Receive Jesus as your Savior
You can receive Jesus right now by faith through prayer. It's a personal decision to invite Him to be the Lord of your life.
"Yet to all who did receive him, to those who believed in his name, he gave the right to become children of God." - John 1:12

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the Young Adults Ministry for?

Our ministry is for all young adults, typically from post-high school through their early 30s. This includes university students, young professionals, entrepreneurs, and those starting families. We welcome everyone looking for community and purpose.

How can I get involved?

The best way to start is by checking out the Connect section to find a small group or join our Discord. From there, you can explore our resources, serving opportunities, and events. Or just use the contact form below to get connected!

What is the BEP Hub?

The Business & Entrepreneurs Hub (BEP) is our dedicated initiative for socio-economic empowerment. It's a platform for networking, mentorship, and collaboration among young professionals and entrepreneurs, featuring programs like the Professionals' Zone and the Freelancer Program.
